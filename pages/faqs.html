FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is auxi.my?

    auxi.my is a mobile app that serves as a collaborative crowdsourcing platform, allowing users to crowdsource their assignments and homework. It connects students who need assistance with knowledgeable helpers who can assist and guide them towards their goals.


  2. How does auxi.my work?

    auxi.my works by leveraging the collective knowledge and expertise of its user community. Users who require assignment help first post their assignment requests, and users with relevant expertise will bid to obtain the assignment. The requesting users can chat with the bidders using our in-app chat or externally (WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, etc) through the bidders' provided contact number. Ultimately, the decision lies solely on the requester to choose who can perform the task.


  3. Is auxi.my available as a mobile app or only as a web platform?

    auxi.my is ONLY available for Android devices and downloadable from Google Play Store only at this moment. In the future, auxi will expand to include iOS devices and available as a web app.


  4. Can I use auxi.my for all subjects and academic levels?

    Yes, auxi.my supports a wide range of subjects and academic levels, including math, science, humanities, languages, computer science, enginnering and many more. It caters to students from schools through university and beyond.


  5. Is auxi.my a free platform?

    Yes, auxi.my offers a free platform to connect users needing help and user looking for extra income. However, we do offer Pro membership that unlocks additional premium features for enhanced functionality and ads-less experience.


  6. How can I sign up for auxi.my?

    To sign up for auxi.my, you can download the mobile app from Google Play Store. You will need to provide some basic information and create an account to get started.


  7. Is my personal information secure on auxi.my?

    Yes, auxi.my takes user privacy and data security seriously. We employ strict measures to safeguard your personal information and ensure confidentiality. We adhere to industry-standard security protocols to protect user data.


  8. Can I choose the expert or user who helps me with my assignment?

    While auxi.my does not offer direct selection of specific experts or users, the platform notifies relevant users with expertise in the subject area of your assignment request. They can choose to volunteer and assist you based on their availability and interest.


  9. Can I earn money by helping others on auxi.my?

    Yes, auxi.my provides an opportunity for knowledgeable users to earn income by assisting students with their assignments. By leveraging your expertise, you can contribute to the auxi community while earning compensation for your assistance.


  10. What if I have a technical issue or need support while using auxi.my?

    If you encounter any technical issues or require support while using auxi, send us an email at auxi.my001@gmail.com, and we will promptly address your concerns and provide guidance.


  11. In the event of a dispute, how can I resolve it?

    Please note that auxi.my acts solely as a platform to connect students with helpers, and we do not process any payments or have direct involvement in transactions between users. We do not take any cut or commision in all transactions. In the event of a dispute, we encourage users to communicate and resolve the issue directly with each other. We recommend open and respectful dialogue to address any concerns or misunderstandings. If a resolution cannot be reached, we suggest seeking mediation or arbitration services that are appropriate for your jurisdiction or seeking legal advice if necessary. Please refer to our terms of service for more information on user responsibilities and dispute resolution.
