Community Guidelines

We're dedicated to creating a collaborative and respectful community where students and assignment helpers can connect and work together. To ensure a positive and productive experience for everyone, we have established the following community guidelines.

  1. Respect and Professionalism
    • Treat all members with respect and professionalism, regardless of their role or background.
    • Refrain from using offensive, abusive, or discriminatory language.
    • Avoid personal attacks or harassment towards other members.
  2. Academic Integrity
    • Promote academic integrity and originality.
    • Do not use the platform for plagiarism or any form of cheating.
    • Seek assistance and guidance while maintaining the integrity of your work
  3. Clear Communication
    • Clearly communicate the requirements and expectations of the assignment or project.
    • Assignment helpers should provide a detailed proposal, outlining their approach and deliverables.
    • Students should clearly articulate their requirements and provide necessary materials to helpers.
  4. Legal and Ethical Conduct
    • Do not exchange copyrighted material, including textbooks, software, or any other protected content.
    • Ensure that assignments do not involve any illegal activities, encourage harm, or violate any laws or regulations.
  5. Confidentiality and Privacy
    • Respect the confidentiality and privacy of all members.
    • Helpers should not disclose any personal or sensitive information provided by students.
    • Students should not share personal information or confidential data that is not necessary for the completion of the task.
  6. Fair Bidding and Pricing
    • Encourage fair competition among assignment helpers.
    • Bids should be based on the complexity, length, and requirements of the assignment.
    • Avoid bid manipulation or collusion to gain an unfair advantage.
  7. Quality Assurance and Accountability
    • Strive to deliver high-quality work and meet the agreed-upon deadlines.
    • Provide constructive feedback to helpers and report any issues promptly.
    • Take responsibility for the work you deliver and address any concerns raised by students.
  8. Platform Use and Abuse
    • Do not engage in spamming, unauthorized advertising, or solicitation on the platform.
    • Report any abuse, misuse, or suspicious activities to the auxi team promptly.
  9. Compliance with Terms of Service
    • Adhere to the platform's terms of service and policies.
    • Violations of the guidelines or terms may result in warnings, suspension, or termination of accounts.
  10. Continuous Improvement
    • We value your feedback and suggestions to improve the platform and our guidelines.
    • Regularly review and update the guidelines to address emerging issues and challenges.
